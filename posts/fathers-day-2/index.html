Father's Day

2012-06-15T11:38:00Z
By pastor

Hello Friends,

This Sunday is Father's Day. If your father is still abiding here on earth make sure to let him know that you love and appreciate him. Whether he is or not, your heavenly Father is alive and well. Make sure you let Him know how thankful you are, if for no other reason, just to be a part of His family!

Gen 18:19 KJV
For I know him, that he will command his children and his household after him, and they shall keep the way of the Lord, to do justice and judgment;

In His Love,

Pastors Frank and Judi
