Overcoming Faith

2011-05-21T11:30:00Z
By admin

Hello Friends,

We are constantly in a spiritual battle. Satan desires to stop us from operating in faith. Because we really are often ignorant of his devices, we cooperate with him in functioning in weak or no faith at all. He is a defeated foe and we become our own worst enemy. Sunday we will begin looking at the persevering Christian. The one who overcomes.

I John 5:4-5
...This is the victory that has overcome the world, even our faith.

Will you be an over-comer?
