Taste and See

2011-04-08T11:33:00Z
By admin

Hello Friends,

The Word says to, "Taste and see that the Lord is good and His mercy endureth forever."

The Lord is good, always, at all times, in all circumstances. When negative or bad things happen, He did not cause them and He is still Good!
You don't "taste and see that the Lord is sometimes good and sometimes bad."

Don't forget tomorrow is the youth and children's yard sell to raise money for camp! Join us at least for a good, fresh hot dog and some wonderful fellowship! Looking forward to seeing you there!
