The Goodness of God

Posted on 2011-09-25T09:30:00Z by admin

Posted in Pastor's Pen

goodness

Hello Friends,
 
Sometimes it is difficult to believe just how good God really is!
 
Rom 2:4NKJV
Or do you despise the riches of His goodness, forbearance, and longsuffering, not knowing that the goodness of God leads you to repentance?
  
It is the goodness of God that leads us into the great exchange.
 
2 Cor 5:21NKJV
For He made Him who knew no sin to be sin for us, that we might become the righteousness of God in Him.
 
 And again it is recorded in Paul's letter to the Corinthian church,
  
2 Cor 8:9NKJV
For you know the grace of our Lord Jesus Christ, that though He was rich, yet for your sakes He became poor, that you through His poverty might become rich.

Jesus took our place that we might assume HIs.
  
God is Good! All the time!
 
Don't forget we have evening worship tonight at 6 P.M. Don't you miss it!
 
Pastors Frank and Judi
