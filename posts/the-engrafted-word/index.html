The Engrafted Word

2011-05-26T11:30:00Z
By admin

Hello Friends,

James 1:21
Wherefore lay apart all filthiness and superfluity of naughtiness, and receive with meekness the engrafted word, which is able to save your souls.

Two things we are instructed to do in this verse, separate ourselves from filthiness and the overflow of wickedness and to receive the engrafted or implanted Word and thereby saving our soul (our mind, will and emotions). The Father has saved our spirits but left our souls up to us. It is a process that requires time and the encouragement of other Christians. Hang in there don't give up! The only way Satan can defeat you is for you to give up and quit.
