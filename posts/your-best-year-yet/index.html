Your Best Year Yet

Posted on 2011-01-08T11:36:00Z by admin

Posted in News & Events

Hello Friends,

We trust that you have found the first weeks of January to be the seed for 2011 becoming your best year yet! You might ask on what evidence or what facts we base this belief on? It comes straight from the mouth of Jesus Himself!

Matt 17:20
And Jesus said unto them, Because of your unbelief: for verily I say unto you, If ye have faith as a grain of mustard seed, ye shall say unto this mountain, Remove hence to yonder place; and it shall remove; and nothing shall be impossible unto you.

If the world can sing, "gloom, despair, and agony on me," how much more should the body of Christ sing of our victory!

John 16:33
These things I have spoken unto you, that in me ye might have peace. In the world ye shall have tribulation: but be of good cheer; I have overcome the world.

In His Love,

Pastors Frank and Judi
