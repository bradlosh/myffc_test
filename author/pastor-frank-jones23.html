To Know Jesus, To Make Jesus Known

Pastors Frank & Judi Jones have lead Faith Family Church for over 20 years. With their leadership we have seen immense life change and growth. We have a passion for people of all ages and from all walks of life to become followers of Christ. Each of our ministries reflect a passion for our community and city.


Every weekend we gather for worship and teaching on Sunday at 10:30am. There are services and classes for your kids during our worship experiences on the weekend and Wednesday evenings at 6:30pm.
